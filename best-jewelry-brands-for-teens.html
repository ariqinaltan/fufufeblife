23 Best Jewelry Brands &amp; Jewelry Trends for Teens 2024

Looking to get your teenager a bit of bling for their birthday? Opt for a piece of teen-approved jewelry from one of their favorite brands. Sure, you could get them some skincare, makeup or other beauty products, but there are not many things more sentimental than something that sparkles. And, if you’re worried about the price tag, there is no need to fret since these Gen-Z-approved brands are affordably priced and arrive in a wide range of styles to fit into your teenager’s wardrobe aesthetic perfectly.

Whether your teen prefers something simple and dainty or loud and colorful, there is a range of jewelry companies with something perfect for Valentine’s Day (and everyday). For example, Mejuri is great for gorgeous dainty (and heart-emblazoned) pieces that look just as expensive as their luxury counterparts, BaubleBar is fabulous for something a bit more personalized (or colorful), Target is a great place to score trends of all kinds (like Kendra Scott!) at reasonable prices and Nordstrom is a tried-and-true spot you can always trust.

On the other hand, Studs makes super trendy earrings with funky styles like those in their NYC collection—that includes a hot pretzel, Metrocard, and more—along with the perfect everyday earrings. And if you’re looking for high-quality pieces that are “beginner luxe”, VRAI is a great choice. To help make the shopping experience a bit simpler, we’ve rounded up the best jewelry brands for teens so you can find a piece that they’ll adore.

  • The Viral Bubble Initial Necklace

    Get your teen the Bubble Initial Necklace that sold 20,000 units in one week. BaubleBar makes affordable, unique pieces that feel out of the box yet approachable, and we can’t help but enthusiastically recommend this whimsical piece (which is available in sparkly pavé, too). This necklace feels more like art (Jeff Koons comes to mind): Get it while it’s 20 percent off! 

    BaubleBar, Bubble Initial Necklace

    $58

  • Kendra Scott x Target

    Kendra Scott necklaces are the stuff of legends, and the brilliant Kendra Scott x Target collection makes the brand even more affordable. Case in point: this Anna Pendant necklace for just $34. The V-Day-appropriate bauble with a Mother of Pearl stone (it comes in a few other options, too) is almost identical to the brand’s main line and the perfect dainty accessory to dress up or down.

    Kendra Scott x Target Anna Pendant Necklace

    $34

  • Kendra Scott

    Any teen who has TikTok saw tons of gorgeous Kendra Scott pieces go viral during Alabama University’s sorority rush (or, RushTok as it was called). It was a phenomenon on the app and something nobody could stop watching — and it made the brand quickly rise in the ranks as a new favorite. Even though the site has a ton of beautiful options, we narrowed it down to the Elisa Gold Pendant Necklace In Iridescent Drusy because it’s something your teen will want to wear all the time.

    Kendra Scott Elisa Gold Pendant Necklace

    $64

  • Kate Spade

    If you’re looking for something whimsical — yet elevated — for your teen, look no further than Kate Spade. Kate Spade’s jewelry line offers unique designs and high quality at affordable prices. We’re specifically eyeing these confetti glitter stud earrings made with a gold-plated base. They’re perfect for everyday wear as well as a formal occasion, like a wedding or bar/bat mitzvah. 

    Kate Spade New York Mini Small Square Studs Earrings

    $20

  • 14K Gold or Sterling Silver Chain Link Band Ring

    For something simple and timeless, get a ring that’ll stand the test of time (and not leave any green marks behind). We’re weirdly impressed by this chain link ring from Amazon that’s both trendy-looking *and* super affordable at $14 a pop (you can even get several and stack ’em). It’s the perfect ring to complement your teen’s other statement pieces.

    Amazon Essentials 14K Gold or Rhodium Plated Sterling Silver Chain Link Band Ring

    $14

    It’s all about chic geometrics with these amethyst-colored Missoma Enamel Haze Large Hoop Earrings. Covered in 18-karat gold plating, the oval drop earrings make a sparkly, dreamy statement. They nail the thick hoop trend with a bit of a twist, so your teen can be trendy and unique at once.

  • Mejuri

    Known for its affordable fine jewelry, Mejuri is for the teen who loves something timeless. The one Valentine’s Gift we have in mind? The Heart Station Necklace. Truthfully, the wearing opportunities are endless, which is why it’s a quintessential addition.

    Mejuri Heart Station Necklace

    $98

  • Pura Vida

    With a ton of unique and trendy pieces at really reasonable prices, Pura Vida is a teen dream! Available in red, black, and rose gold, this Love Hearts Band Ring is to die for (it has L-O-V-E etched inside!) — but check out their entire selection, because it doesn’t disappoint.
